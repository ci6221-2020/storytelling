COVID-19 tests for all aged 13 and up

1 Jul 2020

All aged 13 and above patients who report at clinics with symptoms of acute respiratory infection will be tested for COVID-19.

These are new measures in placed as cases are expected to rise due to higher frequency of close contact when reopening the economy.
